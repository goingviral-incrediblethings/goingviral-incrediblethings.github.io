Let walls fall when you look
And says sometimes we have to play football in
No mans land before returning to the trenches
We can be on the same side
For as long as we want this to last
For hearts a gasp than time may have played
Games
But I look at you and say I am here at last
How long have you been waiting?
You say long enough to know what came before was worth it all
If finding a curiosity that will last a lifetime and adventure in the face of what once made you scared
Is the thing that makes me want to fall in love with you.
