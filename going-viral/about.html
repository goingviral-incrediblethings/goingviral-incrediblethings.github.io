Welcome

Going Viral is a collectively authored archive responding to COVID19 and its effects on people's lives. It is an opportunity for audiences to respond and participate in an uncurated archive, that will exist as an alternative history for many years to come. We made this piece as it felt important to challenge the media's notion of one narrative and push for "We're all in this together". We are using various platforms across the Internet in which our audience can answer questions, respond with creative texts and engage with us the artists through feedback. This project does not have a time period and will last for as long as people choose to engage with it. Once the engagement lessens it will act as an archive for future generations to read and engage with all the many narratives this time has brought us.


We believe the world that comes out of COVID-19 should be one that listens more closely to the desires and needs of its communities. This is our gift, an opportunity to create an online community that will build a collection of all the many stories us as people need to share with each other and the generations that come after us.


Would a friend of family member of yours benefit from Going Viral? Pass our website onto them! Encourage others to get involved to build community and document history during this time.
